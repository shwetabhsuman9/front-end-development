Visit Jamshedpur(The Steel City)

why should you Visit ?

  • It is a popular tourist destination known for its forests, ancient temples and royal palaces.
  • It was ranked as the cleanest city of India in the year 2019.
  • It is one of the first Smart Cities in India along with Naya Raipur.
  • Has a bunch of sightseeings namely : Dimna Lake , Jubilee Park ,Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary, Various Temples etc.

Tourist Attractions

  • Jubilee Park : It is a popular destination for all those who wish to have an outdoor picnic, enjoy some outdoor activities and games or even just to have a relaxing day out with friends and family.


  • Dimna Lake : Whether you want to try water sports, attempt trekking or just chill and get lost in the scenic greenery, this man-made lake will fulfil all your wishes. You can come here anytime between 9 am and 6 pm and enjoy the various interesting activities organised here.


  • Dalma Wildlife Sanctuary : All you wildlife lovers, you must consider paying a visit to this wildlife sanctuary in Jamshedpur. You will get a chance to meet and spot many wild creatures and plants. Watch out for the group of elephants that migrate to this sanctuary every year. You can also enjoy a beautiful view of the Subarnarekha river from atop along with the surrounding greenery. And if you want to explore the sanctuary in depth, we suggest you stay at one of the bamboo huts overnight.
